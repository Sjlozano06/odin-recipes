Honey Chicken Thighs

Juicy and delicous chicken thighs to grow those bi's, tri's, and thighs.

Ingredients:

Chicken & Seasoning

  • 2lbs of boneless & skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 Tbsp of peanut oil
  • 1 tsp of onion powder
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Honey Sauce

  • 70g of honey
  • 50g of sriracha sauce
  • 30g of apple cider vinegar
  • 10g of sesame oil
  • Salt to taste

Directions:

  1. Cut the chicken into bite sized pieces.
  2. Prepare ingredients for sauce and mix in a bowl.
  3. Add peanut oil and chicken to frying pan at medium heat.
  4. Add onion powder,salt, pepper, and garlic.
  5. Cook chicken till brown, then add honey sauce.
  6. Bring to a boil for 1 minute, allowing sauce to thicken.
  7. Remove off heat and serve with any side.
